Our Vision

Enable any SME to launch compliant mobility programs in under 1 hour — zero CS calls, auto-generated mobility documents and first employee invited within 7 days.

Key complications

Why manual onboarding won't scale to meet the Jan 2027 deadline

  • Only 1 in 4 admins arrive ready: 75% of Belgian SME owners lack awareness of FMB pillars, TCO calculation, and legal framework — they need guidance on every configuration decision despite templates being available.
  • Current process doesn't scale: 2 hours manual CS per company × thousands of SMEs = operational collapse. Manual setup blocks the speed needed to capture market before Jan 2027.
  • External advice creates compliance exposure: Companies depend on social secretaries (SD Worx, etc.) for TCO and legal advice, but quality is inconsistent and sometimes legally inaccurate — creating real compliance risk.
  • Decision paralysis on configurations: Even with templates, admins don't know which mobility services to enable/disable (block SNCF? include bike apps in FMB?) or how payment accounts work — they ask for guidance at every step.
